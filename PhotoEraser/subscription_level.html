您可以将同一订阅群组中的每个 App 内购买项目都分配至一个订阅等级。您的订阅等级应该按降序排列，从提供最高等级服务的那一个等级开始。如果订阅提供的服务被视为等同的，则您可以向每个等级添加一个以上的订阅。顾客可以在订阅等级之间调整，即可以升级、降级和跨级。如果支付原价的订阅者需要升级、降级或跨级，则他们需要支付订阅的当前价格，而不是保留的原价。

升级：当顾客从较低等级的订阅切换到更高等级的订阅时。顾客先前的 App 内购买项目金额将会按比例退还到原始的付款方式。新的 App 内购买项目将收取完整价格并立即生效，这会将顾客的续期日期更改为升级日期。

降级：当顾客从较高等级的订阅切换到较低等级的订阅时。在下一个续期日期，会以新费率向顾客收费。

跨级：当顾客在同一等级的订阅间进行切换时。如果 App 内购买项目的时限相同，那么顾客先前的 App 内购买项目金额将会按比例退还到原始的付款方式。新的 App 内购买项目将收取完整价格并立即生效，这会将顾客的续期日期更改为升级日期。如果 App 内购买项目的时限不同，那么跨级将会在顾客的下一个续期日期生效。

You can assign each in-app purchase item in the same subscription group to a subscription level. Your subscription level should be in descending order, starting with the level that provides the highest level of service. If the services provided by the subscription are considered equivalent, you can add more than one subscription to each level. Customers can adjust between subscription levels, which can be upgraded, downgraded, and cross-level. If the subscribers paying the original price need to upgrade, downgrade or cross-level, they will need to pay the current price of the subscription instead of the original price retained.

Upgrade:When a customer switches from a lower-level subscription to a higher-level subscription. The customer's previous in-app purchase item amount will be refunded to the original payment method. The new in-app purchase will be charged the full price and will take effect immediately, which will change the customer's renewal date to the upgrade date.

Downgrade:When a customer switches from a higher level subscription to a lower level subscription. On the next renewal date, customers will be charged at the new rate.

Cross-level:When customers switch between subscriptions of the same level. If the time limit for in-app purchases is the same, then the customer's previous in-app purchases will be refunded to the original payment method. The new in-app purchase will be charged the full price and will take effect immediately, which will change the customer's renewal date to the upgrade date. If the time limit for in-app purchases is different, then the cross-level will take effect on the customer's next renewal date.
